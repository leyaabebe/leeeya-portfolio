My name is Leya Abebe. I received a BS in Business Administration with a Marketing specialization from American University in 2014. I will be graduating with a MA in Web Design & New Media from the Academy of Art University May 2019. A career objective of mine is to work as a user experience or product designer at tech related companies that I find to be innovative and have a personal connection with. I want to continue to grow in craft and produsct thinking so that I can one day become a lead designer.

I've had the privilege to intern with Airbnb as a production designer and to freelance as a designer for Aero, a private jet startup. Outside of work experience, I participate in design related hackathons and try to make a habit of volunteering within the design community and outside of it.

I am most comfortable around user research, testing, information architecture, wireframing, prototyping, and HTML/CSS. I am also comfortable with visual design and motion graphics using After Effects.
