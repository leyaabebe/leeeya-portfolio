Pokémon Go

Pokémon Go is a mobile augmented reality app which brought Pokémon to the real world becomming a global phenomenon in 2016 and is still a widely popular mobile app today.

  • 147 million monthly active users
  • As of 2016, 10.81% of Android devices in the US have Pokémon Go.
  • 5.92% of Android users play daily,
  • 78% were between 18-34 years old,
  • and men, 21-27 year old, were the largest demographic

Compared to the Pokédexes from the GameBoy games and video games, the Pokédex in Pokémon Go combine animations with 3D models of the pokémon along with their stats—a tranformative way to picture the originally 2D drawings of pokémon.
