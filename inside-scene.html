abstract

Purchase or save items of interest from a show or documentary, all from the pause screen.


Inside Scene is a smart tv feature concept aimed at merging Amazon's established rapport as an online retailer into the viewing experience on television via Prime Video.


role

user research, user interviews, wireframing, and visual design


duration

inspiration

Amazon partners with Snapchat to make shopping from visual search accessible.

Being able to explore further what's right in front of you is hwat I want to expand in the FireTV experience. Already users get to find out who from the cast is in the scene at any point in the video. Why not expand it to include looks, merchandise, and more?

competitive analysis

Instagram Shopping's success shows that shopping from personally curated content is...

130 million IG users tap on a shopping post every month. IG has also expanded to allow businesses to setup their checkout process ithin the app, so users don't have to leave IG to compete their purchase(s). Creating this for video would expand upon the concept.

user interview

Here is Toddy's take on what a feature like Inside Scene means for her:

  • Do you use Prime Video?

    • What often inspires Toddy to watch a show or movie is if everyone is talking about the show, if he has the time to wath it, and if it's available. Prime Video is more of a last resort when searching. for movies to watch. She normally streams series on other SVOD sites. If Prime Video weere to structure content based on suggestions, as she is used to on other platforms, she would spend more time on Prime Video. Having to put more effort into search than she has to on other platforms causes her to prioritize Prime Video less.

  • What benefits do you see from this concept?

    • She can see how the project would be beneficial and doesn't have issue with the appearance of more information while the screen is paused. She can imagine that informtaion being accessible before the program, on pause within, and appearing during the credits.

  • Have you any recommendations for this project?

    • "I have to experience it before I indulge in it." Context-specific content should appear when the user pauses, whenever they pause.

"[When Scandal was running] I wanted her whole wardrobe...her long stemmed line glasses especially...I felt like the aesthetic went with my lifestyle..."
