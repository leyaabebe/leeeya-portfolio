user story

Meet Hannah.

17 and excited to start college at a 4-year institution. She is starting her first job as a college student. She wants to be sure to make her money count.

She's on a partial scholarship, but will be working part-time to get experience and help pay for her personal expenses while enrolled. She's appreciative of her family's support and promises not to be too reckless with her spending. However, she's still nervous.

There are so many expenses to keep track of (e.g. textbooks, class fees, subscriptions) and so many things she wants to purchase as well. She would lvoe some help to manage the urges and her spending without calling Mom and Dad for help.

use cases

Register (1/2)

Hannah sets up her profile

Before accessing the tools within the app, users have to complete a brief interest profile. It will help to determine what articles to show first when the user views "Your Insights" later on.

use cases

Onboarding

Hannah visits her Earn, Save, & Spend status

Your Stats is your home screen. It keeps track of your progress in regards to learning about or managing your earnings, savings, or spendings. The more you use the app overall, progress will accrue and update within these categories.

use cases

Use the tools—Journal

Hannah writes a journal entry

To better evaluate how your efforts are affecting your own understanding and management of personal finance, it can be beneficial to write about how you feel at each stage and at later times, review those thoughts. In "Your Tools," there is a journal which you can use just for that.

use cases

Use the tools—Receipts

Hannah scans her receipts

To keep track of how you spend and keep trqack of those receipts you need to use again, you can scan and add receipt photos here.

use cases

Use the tools—Budget

Hannah edits her budget

In the budget tool, Hannah can see the receipts she scanned listed as income or expenses here and/or manually add info, without receipts. Here she can get a broad look of her gains and losses.

use cases

Use the tools—Insights

Hannah reads her insights

A curated list of articles based on her interest profile can be found here. After reading articles, her score in Earn, Save, and Spend goes up depending on the label of the article.
